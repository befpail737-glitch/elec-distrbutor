Infineon CoolSiC MOSFET G2 Now Available

BeiLuo is excited to announce that we are now stocking the latest generation of Infineon CoolSiC MOSFETs. The new G2 series delivers improved performance and efficiency for power electronics applications.

As a core distributor for Infineon Technologies, we are committed to providing our customers with access to the latest semiconductor technologies. The CoolSiC MOSFET G2 series represents a significant advancement in silicon carbide power devices.

Key Features of CoolSiC G2

The second generation of Infineon's CoolSiC MOSFETs offers several improvements over the previous generation:

  • Improved Switching Performance: Reduced switching losses enable higher frequency operation and improved system efficiency
  • Enhanced Gate Oxide Reliability: Advanced gate oxide technology ensures long-term reliability in demanding applications
  • Wide Range of Packages: Available in TO-247, TO-263, and D2PAK packages to suit various application requirements
  • Voltage Ratings: 650V and 1200V options available for different power levels

Applications

The CoolSiC G2 MOSFETs are ideal for a wide range of applications including:

  • Solar inverters and energy storage systems
  • EV charging infrastructure
  • Industrial motor drives
  • Server and telecom power supplies
  • UPS systems

Technical Support

Our FAE team is ready to assist with your CoolSiC G2 design projects. We provide application support, reference designs, and thermal analysis to help you achieve optimal performance in your power electronics designs.

"The CoolSiC G2 series represents the future of power electronics. We're proud to make these cutting-edge devices available to our customers with immediate stock availability."
