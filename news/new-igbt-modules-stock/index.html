Product Update January 5, 2024

New Infineon IGBT Modules Added to Inventory

BeiLuo is pleased to announce the addition of over 200 new Infineon IGBT module part numbers to our inventory. This expansion includes the latest EconoDUAL3 and PrimePACK families, providing our customers with immediate access to high-performance power modules for industrial drives, renewable energy, and EV applications.

Expanded EconoDUAL3 Portfolio

The EconoDUAL3 family remains one of the most popular choices for industrial drives and renewable energy applications. Our expanded inventory now includes:

  • FF600R12ME4: 1200V/600A module with enhanced thermal performance
  • FF450R12ME4: 1200V/450A for medium-power applications
  • FF300R12ME4: 1200V/300A for compact drive designs
  • FF225R12ME4: 1200V/225A for cost-sensitive applications
  • FF600R17ME4: 1700V/600A for high-voltage applications

All EconoDUAL3 modules feature Infineon's latest IGBT4 chipset with:

  • Low switching losses for high-frequency operation
  • Enhanced thermal cycling capability
  • Integrated NTC temperature sensor
  • PressFIT pins for reliable PCB connection
  • UL-certified package design

New PrimePACK Additions

For high-power applications, we've significantly expanded our PrimePACK inventory with the latest 3rd generation modules:

  • FF1400R17IP4: 1700V/1400A for high-power drives and traction
  • FF1000R17IE4: 1700V/1000A with enhanced switching characteristics
  • FF800R17KE3: 1700V/800A for renewable energy inverters
  • FF600R12ME4_B11: 1200V/600A with improved thermal interface

HybridPACK Drive for Automotive

Supporting the growing EV market, we've added new HybridPACK Drive modules specifically designed for automotive traction inverters:

  • FS820R08A6P2B: 750V/820A for 400V EV platforms
  • FS400R07A3E3_H5: 700V/400A compact module for hybrid vehicles
  • FS950R08A6P2B: 750V/950A high-power density solution

These automotive-grade modules feature:

  • AEC-Q100/101 qualified components
  • Extended temperature range (-40°C to 150°C)
  • Enhanced power cycling capability
  • Compact footprint for space-constrained designs

Key Applications

Our expanded IGBT module inventory supports a wide range of applications:

Industrial Motor Drives

From 1kW servo drives to 1MW industrial inverters, our IGBT modules provide the performance and reliability needed for modern motor control systems. The EconoDUAL3 family is particularly well-suited for:

  • Variable frequency drives (VFD)
  • Servo drives and motion control
  • HVAC and compressor drives
  • Pump and fan applications

Renewable Energy

Power conversion for solar and wind applications demands high efficiency and reliability. Our IGBT modules enable:

  • Solar inverters (residential to utility-scale)
  • Wind turbine power converters
  • Energy storage systems
  • Grid-tied converters

Electric Vehicles

The HybridPACK Drive modules are optimized for EV traction inverters, supporting:

  • Battery electric vehicles (BEV)
  • Plug-in hybrid vehicles (PHEV)
  • Commercial EVs (buses, trucks)
  • Electric two-wheelers

Power Supplies & UPS

High-power IGBT modules enable efficient power conversion for:

  • Uninterruptible power supplies (UPS)
  • Industrial welding equipment
  • Induction heating systems
  • SMPS and DC-DC converters

Technical Support

BeiLuo's FAE team is available to support your IGBT module selection and application design:

  • Thermal simulation: Assistance with heatsink selection and thermal design
  • Gate driver recommendations: Matching gate drivers for optimal switching performance
  • PCB layout guidance: Best practices for power module integration
  • Reference designs: Access to proven inverter and converter designs

Availability & Lead Times

All newly added IGBT modules are available for immediate shipment from our warehouse. Key benefits include:

  • Same-day shipping for orders placed before 3 PM
  • No minimum order quantity (MOQ) restrictions
  • Competitive volume pricing available
  • Technical documentation and samples available
