Company News January 12, 2024

BeiLuo Expands FAE Team to Better Serve Customers

BeiLuo is excited to announce the expansion of our Field Application Engineering (FAE) team with the addition of three highly experienced engineers. This strategic expansion strengthens our technical support capabilities and reinforces our commitment to providing exceptional service to our customers.

As a leading electronic components distributor, we understand that technical support is just as important as product availability. Our expanded FAE team brings deep expertise in automotive electronics, power conversion, and industrial automation applications.

Meet Our New FAE Team Members

Our three new field application engineers bring a combined 25+ years of experience in semiconductor applications and system design:

  • Automotive Power Electronics Specialist: 10 years of experience in EV powertrain design, BMS systems, and onboard charging applications. Expert in Infineon SiC and IGBT modules for automotive use.
  • Industrial Drives & Automation Expert: 8 years of experience in motor control, servo drives, and industrial automation systems. Specializes in gate drivers and power module integration.
  • Renewable Energy Applications Engineer: 7 years of experience in solar inverters, energy storage systems, and EV charging infrastructure. Expert in high-power module applications.

Enhanced Technical Support Services

With our expanded FAE team, BeiLuo now offers comprehensive technical support services including:

  • Design Review & Consultation: Expert review of your power electronics designs and recommendations for optimal component selection
  • Application Support: Hands-on assistance with gate driver configuration, thermal management, and PCB layout optimization
  • Reference Designs: Access to proven reference designs and evaluation boards for rapid prototyping
  • Failure Analysis: Expert analysis of field failures and recommendations for design improvements
  • Training & Workshops: Customized technical training for your engineering team on specific product families

Focus on Key Application Areas

Our expanded FAE team will focus on supporting the following high-growth application areas:

Electric Vehicle (EV) Applications

With the rapid growth of the EV market, our automotive FAE specialist will provide expert support for:

  • Traction inverter design using Infineon HybridPACK modules
  • Battery management systems (BMS) using AURIX microcontrollers
  • Onboard chargers and DC-DC converters
  • Thermal management and cooling solutions

Renewable Energy & Storage

Our renewable energy specialist will support customers developing:

  • Solar inverters and energy storage systems
  • EV charging infrastructure (Level 2 and DC fast charging)
  • Grid-tied energy conversion systems
  • Battery energy storage systems (BESS)

Industrial Automation

Our industrial applications expert will assist with:

  • Servo drives and motor control systems
  • Variable frequency drives (VFD)
  • UPS and power quality systems
  • Welding and induction heating equipment

Commitment to Customer Success

"Our customers' success is our success," said the Technical Director at BeiLuo. "By expanding our FAE team, we're investing in the resources needed to help our customers overcome technical challenges and bring their products to market faster. Whether you're designing your first power converter or optimizing a high-volume production design, our FAE team is here to help."
