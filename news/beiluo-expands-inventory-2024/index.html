Company News January 20, 2024

BeiLuo Announces Major Inventory Expansion for 2024

BeiLuo is pleased to announce a significant expansion of our inventory, adding over 5,000 new part numbers from leading semiconductor manufacturers. This strategic expansion strengthens our position as a leading electronic components distributor in China and enhances our ability to serve customers with genuine original stock.

The inventory expansion focuses on high-demand product categories including IGBT modules, SiC power devices, automotive microcontrollers, and industrial sensors. Key additions include the latest generation of Infineon CoolSiC MOSFETs, expanded EconoDUAL3 and PrimePACK IGBT module offerings, and AURIX automotive MCUs.

Expanded Product Lines

The 2024 inventory expansion includes significant additions across multiple product categories:

  • Power Modules: 1,200+ new IGBT and SiC module part numbers from Infineon, including the latest HybridPACK Drive and CoolSiC G2 families
  • Automotive MCUs: 800+ AURIX TC3xx and TC4xx series microcontrollers for next-generation automotive applications
  • Industrial Sensors: 600+ magnetic and current sensor products for industrial automation
  • Gate Drivers: 400+ isolated and non-isolated gate driver ICs for power applications
  • MOSFETs: 2,000+ low, medium, and high-voltage MOSFETs for various applications

Strategic Partnerships

This expansion is made possible through strengthened partnerships with leading semiconductor manufacturers. As an authorized Infineon distributor, BeiLuo continues to deepen our relationship with one of the world's leading semiconductor companies.

"Our expanded inventory reflects our commitment to providing customers with reliable access to genuine electronic components," said the Sales Director at BeiLuo. "In today's market, having deep inventory of critical components is essential for our customers' supply chain security."

Enhanced Services

Along with the inventory expansion, BeiLuo is enhancing our technical support capabilities. Our FAE team has grown to include additional specialists in power electronics and automotive applications, ensuring customers receive expert guidance for their designs.

Key service enhancements include:

  • 24-hour quote response for all stocked items
  • Same-day shipping for orders placed before 3 PM
  • Extended technical support hours
  • Enhanced BOM analysis and alternative sourcing services

About BeiLuo

BeiLuo is a leading electronic components distributor in China, specializing in power semiconductors, microcontrollers, and sensors from world-leading manufacturers. With over 15 years of industry experience, we provide genuine original stock, competitive pricing, and comprehensive technical support to customers worldwide.

Our commitment to quality is demonstrated through our ISO 9001:2023 certification and direct authorization from major semiconductor manufacturers including Infineon Technologies.

"Our mission is to be the most reliable partner for electronic components sourcing. This inventory expansion is a significant step toward fulfilling that promise to our customers."
