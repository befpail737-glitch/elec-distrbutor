Who We Are

Shandong Beiluo Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd is a premier electronic components distributor headquartered in Shandong, China. We specialize in power semiconductors, microcontrollers, and sensors from industry-leading manufacturers including Infineon Technologies, ON Semiconductor, STMicroelectronics, and Texas Instruments.

Our commitment to providing genuine original stock, competitive pricing, and comprehensive technical support has made us a trusted partner for customers worldwide. We serve diverse industries including automotive, industrial automation, renewable energy, and consumer electronics.

With years of industry experience, we understand the critical importance of supply chain reliability. Our extensive inventory and strong relationships with manufacturers ensure that our customers have access to the components they need, when they need them.
